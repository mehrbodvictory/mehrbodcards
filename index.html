How to play

Draft. Units come in four tiers: Blue → Green → Red → Orange.

Merge. Drag one card onto another to fuse. Tiers add together (capped at Orange).

Blue Logic. If you have 4+ Blues on board, you must merge them before taking any other action.

Attack. Damage is simultaneous. Both players assign targets at the same time.
