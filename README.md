# mehrbodcards
A vibe coded card game.

card game that is able to be hosted on GitHub pages.must be multiplayer and have a vs bot option. games rule is you have 12 cards per match. 4 are spells 2 are chips. cards can be merged, each card is either 1 (blue) 2 (green) 3 (red) or 4 (orange). for example a blue and green make a red card. HP and DMG scale as card gets stronger. Chip slots are on cards after blue. all blue cards have 1 hp 1 dmg 1 sp. some cards have abilities for example when placed deal 2 damage to a selected card.
Summary
Build a responsive browser card battler (playable on both desktop and mobile) hosted on GitHub Pages with peer-to-peer multiplayer and a vs-bot mode. Each match has 12-card drafting per player (spells and chips are separate and do not count in the 12-card limit). Cards are colored by value: blue (1), green (2), red (3), orange (4). Blue cards defend infinitely while they exist, with defense limits based on color: blue defends infinitely; green defends twice; red defends once. When defending, the defended card becomes invincible and cannot attack. Once a player has no non-blue cards left in their deck, their blue cards can no longer defend. Players place or merge during the placement round (merges happen only in placement, never during attack). When forced to merge due to max blue cards on board, a valid merge is always available (e.g., 3 blues can merge into 1 red even if no greens exist), and cascades should follow by repeatedly applying forced-merge requirements as needed until not forced. During the attack round, both players select attack targets simultaneously (no turn-taking); then attacks are executed and damage/abilities resolve according to the timing rules: card effects that trigger on death activate immediately when the card dies, and if a card was meant to attack its target but dies before attacking, it still attacks later only after the place round starts again (i.e., dead cards go to the death pile and then are handled in the next place-round cycle per the rules). Goal: wipe out all opponent cards. If both players have zero cards remaining and their boards/decks are empty, a fail-safe decides the winner by counting which player has more unused spells and chips remaining in their decks. Spells and chips can be used at any point (during placement or attack) and can target cards immediately when played. Provide vs-bot with 4 difficulty modes and P2P multiplayer with deterministic outcomes via a shared match seed for all random events/reshuffles/draws.
Highlights
Responsive gameplay UI that supports both touch (mobile) and mouse/keyboard (desktop) on GitHub Pages
Peer-to-peer multiplayer with seeded RNG so draws/merges resolve identically on both peers (no desync)
Clear, deterministic placement/merge rules: merges only during placement; forced merge handling always finds a legal merge; merge availability never stalls the game
Attack resolution and death-trigger timing locked: simultaneous attacks, damage → deaths → immediate on-death abilities; wiped-then-fallback rule uses unused spells/chips when both sides hit zero
